Million Veteran Program, 233,792 veterans (91.6% male, mean age 65.6), egg intake by FFQ, mean 3.3-year follow-up, 5,740 ischemic and 423 hemorrhagic strokes. Clear dose-response for ischemic stroke by egg-intake frequency vs <1/month: HR 1.10 (1-3/month), 1.09 (1/week), 1.10 (2-4/week), 1.16 (5-6/week), 1.20 (1/day), 1.22 (≥2/day) — a positive association with ischemic but not hemorrhagic stroke (P-trend for hemorrhagic = 0.36).
relevance_note: A dose-response harm signal for a stroke subtype specifically, in the same veteran cohort as S-66's CAD result — together the MVP's two hard-endpoint results both point toward harm at higher intake, though from a short (3.3-year) follow-up window prone to reverse causation.
