O-94 — Controlled inpatient depletion-repletion design (1 wk 500 mg/day choline run-in, 3 wk deficient vs control, 1 wk repletion). Demonstrates that tissue choline stores deplete on a choline-free diet despite endogenous de novo phosphatidylcholine synthesis.
