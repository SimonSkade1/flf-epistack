The direct empirical measurement-error base rate for FFQ-derived energy in the OPEN validation sample. Because egg-intake frequency in the major cohorts is FFQ-derived, this quantifies the magnitude of self-report error underlying those exposure variables. The FFQ-vs-24HR gap shows the instrument, not just the respondents, drives much of the bias.
