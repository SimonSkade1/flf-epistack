No interaction was found for age, BMI, physical activity, smoking, hypertension, hypercholesterolemia, family history of MI, statin use, or the Alternative Healthy Eating Index."

The only significant effect-modifier found among the tested variables was type 2 diabetes status -- relevant to the 'for whom' question -- although neither diabetes stratum on its own showed a significant egg-CVD association.
