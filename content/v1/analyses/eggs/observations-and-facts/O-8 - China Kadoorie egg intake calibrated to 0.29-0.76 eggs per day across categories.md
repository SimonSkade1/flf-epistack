Frequency categories and calibrated mean intake (egg/day): never/rarely 0.29; 1-3 days/month 0.36; 1-3 days/week 0.46; 4-6 days/week 0.56; daily 0.76. Reproducibility of the frequency measure (age/sex-adjusted Spearman r) was 0.58 over a mean 5.4-month interval (n=926). This exposure ceiling means the paper's dose-response says nothing about intakes above ~1 egg/day.
