Reference (lowest-risk) category 3-<6 eggs/week. Cohort-stratified Cox regression, multivariable-adjusted. Intermediate category HRs (1-<3/week, 6-<10/week) were not accessible (paywalled full text); the reported extremes plus the interior reference establish the U-shape and its thresholds.
