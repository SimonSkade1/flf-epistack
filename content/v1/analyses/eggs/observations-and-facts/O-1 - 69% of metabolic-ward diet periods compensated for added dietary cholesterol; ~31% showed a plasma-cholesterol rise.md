Direct-measurement metabolic-ward design: fractional cholesterol absorption and sterol synthesis (in isolated blood mononuclear leukocytes) were measured in each of 75 twelve-week diet periods, alongside plasma lipids. The 69/31 split is a per-period compensation frequency, not a per-subject genotype count (exact subject n not recoverable from the accessible text).
