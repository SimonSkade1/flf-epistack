---
The egg-specific HDL rise is isolated by the substitute group (same CRD background, no eggs) showing no HDL change - so the increment is attributable to the eggs, not the diet background alone (though the CRD itself raises HDL, making this a same-background comparison rather than egg-vs-low-fat control).
