The firmly-established, uncontested directional fact that memory-based dietary self-report under-reports energy versus an unbiased recovery biomarker. It rests on the broad doubly-labeled-water validation literature, not on OPEN's dataset alone; OPEN (see the paper-derived observations) is one primary quantification of it. Evidence-like because it constrains how much confidence any FFQ-based egg-intake exposure variable deserves.
