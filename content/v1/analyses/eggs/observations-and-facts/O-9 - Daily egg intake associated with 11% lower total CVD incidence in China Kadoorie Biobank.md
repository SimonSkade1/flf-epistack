Fully-adjusted (stratified Cox on survey site + birth cohort; attained age as timescale; adjusted for age, sex, education, income, marital status, alcohol, smoking, physical activity, BMI, waist-hip ratio, hypertension, aspirin, family CVD history, multivitamins, dietary pattern). Category HRs (95% CI) vs never/rarely: 1-3 days/month 0.97 (0.95-1.00); 1-3 days/week 0.92 (0.90-0.94); 4-6 days/week 0.90 (0.87-0.93); daily 0.89 (0.87-0.92). Per +1 egg/week: 0.97 (0.96-0.98).
