## Why this is evidence
The confounder-correlation structure (US egg intake co-occurring with red/processed meat, refined grains, sugary drinks, smoking, higher BMI) is the premise H-9's residual-confounding account requires; H-8 (genuine causal effect) and H-51 (uniform effect) do not need it. Its presence in the US cohorts raises H-9 relative to the two.
