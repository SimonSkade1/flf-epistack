## Why this is evidence
A 4-week, 4-egg/day habitual dose leaving fasting TMAO flat in normal-renal adults is what H-29 predicts and what H-42/H-56 (a real production rise / a chronic steady-state elevation) do not. The pre-selection to normal renal function leaves H-30's impaired-renal case as the open condition rather than testing it.
