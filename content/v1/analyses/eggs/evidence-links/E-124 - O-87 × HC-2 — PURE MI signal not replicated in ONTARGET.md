## Why this is evidence

The single protective clinical signal (MI in PURE) fails to replicate in ONTARGET/TRANSCEND and is flagged by the authors as a likely multiple-testing artefact. A genuine population effect (H-22) should replicate across cohorts; H-7 (confounded) and H-50 (chance/artefact) predict exactly this non-replication of an isolated protective signal.
