## Why this is evidence
A significant egg-T2D interaction with overall diet quality shows the effect is contingent on background diet, cutting against a fixed intrinsic property (H-17) and supporting background-diet-indexing (H-38). Direction (more harmful at lower diet quality) is opposite to H-38's specific refined-carb-displacement mechanism, so it evidences background-diet contingency generally more than H-38's particular direction.
