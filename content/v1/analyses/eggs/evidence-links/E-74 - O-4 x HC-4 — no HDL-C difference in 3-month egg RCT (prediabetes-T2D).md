## Why this is evidence
H-4 predicts no adverse blood-lipid effect of ~12 eggs/week in prediabetes/T2D (a null is expected); the harm-mechanism member H-33 predicts egg-derived cholesterol is handled more atherogenically in this population, i.e. an adverse lipid shift. A pre-specified null HDL-C is the safe pole's prediction, not the harm pole's.
