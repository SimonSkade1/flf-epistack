## Why this is evidence

Egg intake in PURE varies regionally (5.6/week in China to 1.4/week in South Asia) and no region reaches meaningfully above 1 egg/day. This directly documents H-50's differential-exposure-range mechanism for apparent regional effect-heterogeneity; H-22 and H-7 are agnostic to intake range and do not predict it.
