## Why this is evidence
Co-ingested egg white does not blunt free-choline's TMAO rise, ruling out egg-white antimicrobial peptides as the reason whole eggs are TMAO-null. That leaves choline form itself (H-28) as the explanation and disfavours H-56 alternatives in which an egg-matrix inhibitor suppresses conversion.
