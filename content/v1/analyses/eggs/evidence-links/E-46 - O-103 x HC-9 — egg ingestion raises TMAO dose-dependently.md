## Why this is evidence
A dose-dependent plasma/urine TMAO rise with ~11-15% of egg choline converted directly supports H-42 (egg choline measurably enters the TMAO pathway) and is against H-28's form-limited-negligible reading and H-29's no-rise reading. Measured postprandially on a low-choline background, so it does not by itself settle the fasting/steady-state question the other members turn on.
