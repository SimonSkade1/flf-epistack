## Why this is evidence
The crux observation. "Eggs rank near processed meat" (a fixed bottom-of-ranking position) supports H-17's intrinsic unhealthiness; that egg->unprocessed-red-meat still lowers risk contradicts H-23's "eggs preferable to red meat." That the sign depends on the comparator also supports comparator-contingency (H-18) over a comparator-free reading.
