## Why this is evidence
The US-positive / Europe-null / Asia-inverse gradient (p-interaction 0.01) is the signature both H-8 (a genuinely region-dependent causal effect) and H-9 (region-varying residual confounding) predict; it does not separate those two. It discriminates against H-51, whose uniform-across-regions effect predicts no such gradient.
