## Why this is evidence
If the sign and size of removing eggs depend entirely on the replacement food, no fixed egg-intrinsic exposure (cholesterol H-26 or fat quality H-3) is carrying a stable effect. Comparator-dependence favours H-54 (dietary-pattern/substitution artefact) over both H-26 and H-3.
