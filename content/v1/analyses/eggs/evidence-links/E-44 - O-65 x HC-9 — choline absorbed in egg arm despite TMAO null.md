## Why this is evidence
Circulating choline rose in the egg arm just as in the TMAO-raising free-choline arms, so the egg TMAO null is not malabsorption or non-compliance - the block sits at the gut-microbial conversion step. That localization is what H-28 (form governs microbial TMA production) predicts and what a naive amount-driven reading behind H-42 does not.
