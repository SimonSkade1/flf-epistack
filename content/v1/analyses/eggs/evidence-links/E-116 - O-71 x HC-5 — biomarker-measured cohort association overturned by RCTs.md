## Why this is evidence
A cohort association measured with an objective biochemical assay (beta-carotene) that RCTs exclude shows nutritional-cohort associations can be pure confounding even without measurement error — supporting H-54's 'no real operative exposure / artefact' branch over a genuine egg-intrinsic agent (H-26 or H-3).
