## Why this is evidence
Egg choline is converted to TMAO dose-dependently in humans, giving a non-cholesterol egg component with a plausible atherogenic route — the 'other operative exposure' H-54 posits. H-26/H-3 attribute egg's effect to cholesterol/fat, not choline, so this discriminates H-54 from both.
