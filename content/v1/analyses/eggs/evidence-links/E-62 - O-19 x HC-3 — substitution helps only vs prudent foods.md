## Why this is evidence
A genuine egg-specific causal effect (H-8, or a positive H-51) predicts lower modelled T2D risk when eggs are replaced by any roughly-neutral food. The observed reduction only when substituting prudent foods (whole grains, nuts, dairy) and no change versus other animal proteins, fish, legumes or refined-pattern foods is the marker signature H-9 predicts: eggs carry no intrinsic effect, so swaps within the Western basket are neutral and the benefit tracks the replacing food. Direction: favors H-9 over H-8.
