## Why this is evidence
Higher egg eaters cluster with established CVD risk factors (more red/processed meat, smoking, higher BMI, more T2D). This confounding structure is exactly what H-21 (null) needs to explain away the modest crude positive egg-CVD association as non-causal, and it is the mechanism by which an apparent harm signal (H-27) could be manufactured without any causal effect. Under H-6 (protection) one would not expect a positive crude association at all. The observation thus discriminates the null from the harmful reading by supplying the confounding pathway; it is agnostic between them only on residual (unmeasured) confounding.
