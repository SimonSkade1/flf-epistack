## Why this is evidence
H-27 frames egg harm as operating through egg cholesterol raising atherogenic lipids; flat LDL/total-cholesterol/ApoB across egg categories (plus slightly lower blood pressure) means that necessary first link is not engaged, lowering P(harm) under H-27 and making the clinical null mechanistically coherent under H-21. H-6 (protection) is mildly consistent with the lower blood pressure. It discriminates the cholesterol-mediated harm member from the null/protective members via the absent intermediate.
