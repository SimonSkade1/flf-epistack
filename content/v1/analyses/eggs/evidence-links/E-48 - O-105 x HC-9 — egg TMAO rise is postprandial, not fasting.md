## Why this is evidence
This is the reconciling discriminator across the cluster: the Miller egg rise is a single-meal postprandial flux, not a fasting steady-state change. It is exactly the postprandial excursion H-42/H-43 describe, and it is compatible with H-29's fasting null (Wilcox), so it separates "postprandial rise" (H-42) and "benign postprandial excursion" (H-43) from "no fasting/steady-state rise" (H-29) rather than contradicting the latter.
