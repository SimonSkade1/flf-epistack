## Why this is evidence
Inflammation (hsCRP) and LDL oxidation did not move with the TMAO rise, which is precisely H-43's stated basis for calling the egg-induced excursion clinically benign. H-42 asserts the rise happens but is silent on harm markers, so this observation separates H-43 from the other members. (n=6, 24-h window limit power.)
