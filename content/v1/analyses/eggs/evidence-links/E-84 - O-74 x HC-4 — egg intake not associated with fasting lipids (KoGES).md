## Why this is evidence
H-33 routes egg harm through deranged cholesterol transport, predicting an egg-lipid association; the cohort shows none across intake quartiles, so the diabetic CVD signal (O-73, same study) is not accompanied by the lipid derangement H-33 requires — favouring H-4's lipid-neutrality and pressuring the H-33 mechanism.
