## Why this is evidence

A strong protective cohort mortality association (RR 0.69), measured with an objective biomarker (so not measurement error), is fully excluded by RCTs — a template for a protective observational signal being healthy-user/selection confounding rather than causal. This raises H-7 (the Asian inverse is confounding) and H-50 (artefact) relative to H-22 (a real protective regional effect).
