## Why this is evidence
Choline delivered in egg's phospholipid form (phosphatidylcholine) at a matched dose does not raise TMAO or platelet reactivity — the egg-choline candidate of H-54 is not operative — leaving cholesterol (H-26) as the egg-intrinsic candidate.
