## Why this is evidence
H-31 reads near-universal food-mortality significance as a signature of pervasive confounding among mutually correlated dietary exposures plus flexible, non-prespecified analysis (single-food effects non-identifiable); H-41 reads it as either genuine modest whole-diet effects or a pattern that well-conducted analysis manages.
