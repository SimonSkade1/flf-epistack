## Why this is evidence
A reversible ALT rise — a sign of incipient liver dysfunction — appearing only in the choline-deficient arm is the organ-dysfunction deficiency sign H-39 (essential) predicts a choline-free diet produces; H-48 (not essential: synthesis suffices, no reliable deficiency) predicts no such dysfunction.
