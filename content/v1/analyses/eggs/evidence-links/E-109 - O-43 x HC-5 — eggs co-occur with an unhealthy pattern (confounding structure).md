## Why this is evidence
Higher egg intake co-occurs with an unhealthier diet-lifestyle pattern, so the raw egg-CVD association could be residual confounding rather than any egg component. This supports H-54's artefact branch over a genuine egg-intrinsic agent (H-26 cholesterol or H-3 fat).
