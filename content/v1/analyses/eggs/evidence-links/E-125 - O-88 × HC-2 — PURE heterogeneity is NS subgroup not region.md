## Why this is evidence

Within PURE the only heterogeneity signal is a non-significant prevalent-CVD subgroup (p-interaction 0.24), NOT region, and results were consistent across geographic regions; the subgroup did not reproduce. H-22 (region-modification) predicts a regional interaction should be the salient one; H-50 (chance / non-significant interactions) predicts the apparent modification is a fragile non-regional subgroup as observed.
