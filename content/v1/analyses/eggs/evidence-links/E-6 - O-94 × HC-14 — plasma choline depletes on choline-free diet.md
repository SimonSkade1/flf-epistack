## Why this is evidence
H-39 (essential) predicts choline pools deplete on a choline-free diet because de novo PEMT synthesis cannot cover the requirement; H-48 (not essential) predicts endogenous synthesis suffices to buffer plasma choline near-normal. A ~30% fall in plasma choline/phosphatidylcholine is the direction H-39 predicts and H-48 predicts against.
