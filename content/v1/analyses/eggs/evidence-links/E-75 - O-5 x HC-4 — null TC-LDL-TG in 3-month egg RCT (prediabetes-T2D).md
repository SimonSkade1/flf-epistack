## Why this is evidence
H-33 posits egg cholesterol is more atherogenic in diabetics, predicting raised total/LDL cholesterol at high egg intake; H-4 predicts no adverse lipids. A null total-C/LDL/TG in the exact at-risk population favours H-4 over H-33 (and the wider harm pole).
