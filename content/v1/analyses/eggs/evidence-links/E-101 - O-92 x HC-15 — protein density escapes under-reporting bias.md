## Why this is evidence
Absolute protein is under-reported ~30% yet protein density (a ratio) barely at all. H-41 reads this as the empirical basis for energy-adjustment/ratio measures removing most measurement bias (the error is manageable); H-31 emphasizes the large absolute-intake error. Discriminates toward the defence pole.
