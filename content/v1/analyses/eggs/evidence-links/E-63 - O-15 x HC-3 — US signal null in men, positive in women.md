## Why this is evidence
H-51's uniform causal effect predicts a consistent positive association across sexes; the null in HPFS men (1.07, CI crosses 1) is awkward for it. A fragile, sex-varying US signal is also more consistent with H-9 (residual confounding, which can vary by sex/cohort) than with a stable region-level causal effect (H-8).
