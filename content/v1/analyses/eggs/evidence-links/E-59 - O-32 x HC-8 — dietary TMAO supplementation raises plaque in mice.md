## Why this is evidence
Direct TMAO supplementation raising aortic plaque, with plaque tracking plasma TMAO and not lipids, is predicted by H-15 and H-16 (a real causal mechanism). Under H-45 (TMAO a non-causal marker) manipulating TMAO itself should not increase atherosclerosis. Separates the marker pole from the two mechanism poles; does not by itself separate H-15 from H-16.
