## Why this is evidence
A ~15% fall in serum cholesterol only in the deficient arm reflects impaired hepatic VLDL/lipid export when choline is withdrawn — a metabolic deficiency sign H-39 (essential) predicts; H-48 (not essential) predicts endogenous synthesis prevents any such derangement.
