## Why this is evidence
FMO3 (the TMA->TMAO enzyme) and an FMO3-region SNP co-segregating with lesion area and plasma TMAO is a natural-experiment / Mendelian-randomization-type link between TMAO production and atherosclerosis. Expected under H-15 and H-16; not under H-45, where TMAO is a marker and a TMAO-generating locus should not track disease. Separates the marker pole from the mechanism poles.
