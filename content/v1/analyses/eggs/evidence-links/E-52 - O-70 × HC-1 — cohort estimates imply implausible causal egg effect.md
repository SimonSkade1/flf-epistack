## Why this is evidence
If the harmful egg cohort estimate (H-27) or a large protective one (H-6) were lifelong-causal, the implied magnitude (about -6 years of life expectancy per daily egg, alongside mutually incompatible figures for other foods) is implausible — which is the expected consequence under H-21/H-49, where such cohort associations are confounded/non-causal rather than causal. It therefore lowers P(obs) under a strong causal reading (H-6, H-27) relative to the null/residual, discriminating the causal-verdict members from the null.
