## Why this is evidence
Eggs were less LDL-favourable than the energy-matched high-carb comparator — the adverse-lipid direction H-33 predicts (egg cholesterol more atherogenic in this at-risk group) and counter to H-4's lipid-safety claim. H-19 (glycemic) is silent on LDL.
