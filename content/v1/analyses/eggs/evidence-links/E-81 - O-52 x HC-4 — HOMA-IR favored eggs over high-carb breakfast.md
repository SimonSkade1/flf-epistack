## Why this is evidence
A large HOMA-IR advantage for eggs over the high-carb breakfast is exactly H-19's prediction (substituting eggs for high-carb foods improves insulin resistance) and runs against the net-harm pole (H-24/H-33); H-4 predicts only neutrality, not benefit.
