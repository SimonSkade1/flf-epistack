## Why this is evidence

Discriminates both facets of the thesis from the residual H-58. The 77% attribution to Grade A shell eggs is strongly predicted by H-12 (eggs the principal vehicle) and contradicts the H-58 branch where eggs are not the principal vehicle. The implicated eggs being intact, uncracked and properly refrigerated is predicted by H-11 (internal/trans-ovarian route, uncontrollable by handling) and contradicts the H-58 branch where transmission is external/shell contamination controllable by handling — which requires cracked or soiled eggs.
