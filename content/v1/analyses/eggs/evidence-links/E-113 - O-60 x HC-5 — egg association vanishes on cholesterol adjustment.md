## Why this is evidence
The egg association becoming non-significant once dietary cholesterol is in the model is the signature of cholesterol as the mediator (H-26). A non-cholesterol egg component or pure dietary-pattern confounding (H-54) would not be removed specifically by cholesterol adjustment; H-3 makes cholesterol a minor lever, so full attenuation to it is less expected.
