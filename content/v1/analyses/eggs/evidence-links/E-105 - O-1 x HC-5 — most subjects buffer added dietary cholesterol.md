## Why this is evidence
H-3 (fat quality the larger determinant) predicts most people homeostatically compensate for added dietary cholesterol, making cholesterol quantity a weak lever on plasma cholesterol; H-26 (cholesterol content operative) predicts dietary cholesterol reliably raises it. 69% compensating favours H-3 and downweights H-26.
