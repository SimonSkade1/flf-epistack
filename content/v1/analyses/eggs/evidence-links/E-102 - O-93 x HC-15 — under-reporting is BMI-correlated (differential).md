## Why this is evidence
Under-reporting rises with BMI - a person-specific component correlated with a determinant of many outcomes. H-31 reads this as violating the non-differential/classical measurement-error model that standard attenuation correction assumes, so the error is not simply correctable; H-41's manageability claim rests on the error being non-differential. Discriminates.
