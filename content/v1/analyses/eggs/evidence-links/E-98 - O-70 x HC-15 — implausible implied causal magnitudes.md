## Why this is evidence
Taken as lifelong causal the meta-analyzed cohort HRs imply individually implausible, mutually incompatible magnitudes. H-31 takes this reductio as marking the estimates bias-driven rather than causal; H-41 treats the lifelong-causal extrapolation as a strawman and the underlying estimates as valid but not meant to be extrapolated that way.
