## Why this is evidence
A prospective human association predicting incident MI/stroke/death is strongly expected under H-16 (human causal factor) and under H-45 (marker of renal status / unhealthy diet), but only weakly under H-15, whose human translation is explicitly open. Raises H-16 and H-45 relative to H-15; being an untargeted, thinly-adjusted angiography sub-cohort it does not by itself separate causal H-16 from marker H-45.
