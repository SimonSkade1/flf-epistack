## Why this is evidence
A strong protective cohort association measured with an objective biochemical assay (so dietary self-report error is bypassed) that randomized trials nonetheless exclude. H-31 reads this as proof that confounding/selection, not merely measurement error, generates nutritional-cohort associations, so they cannot carry causal weight; H-41 confines it to single-nutrient supplement trials (unrepresentative of whole-diet cohort inference) and points to trial triangulation as the corrective.
