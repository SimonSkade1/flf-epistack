## Why this is evidence
A causal manipulation: lowering plasma TMAO ~100-fold via antibiotics completely inhibits the choline-induced 3-fold atherosclerosis. Predicted by H-15 and H-16 (TMAO on the causal path). Under H-45 (marker), removing TMAO should not remove the plaque, so this is not expected. Separates the marker pole from the mechanism poles.
