## Why this is evidence

PURE is a 50-country cohort that includes Asian and Chinese participants and adjusts for region (center random intercepts), yet finds a null egg-CVD association explicitly consistent across geographic regions. H-22 predicts an inverse Asian signal should re-appear; H-7 and H-50 predict the Kadoorie inverse should NOT reproduce in a better-controlled, region-adjusted multi-region cohort.
