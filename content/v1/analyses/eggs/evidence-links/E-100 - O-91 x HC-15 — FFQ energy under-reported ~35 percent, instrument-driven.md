## Why this is evidence
H-31 reads the large, FFQ-specific (~2x the 24HR) energy under-reporting as evidence the FFQ exposure instrument itself is too degraded to trust; H-41 concedes the magnitude but treats it as non-differential and recoverable by biomarker calibration. Discriminates on how damning the error is.
