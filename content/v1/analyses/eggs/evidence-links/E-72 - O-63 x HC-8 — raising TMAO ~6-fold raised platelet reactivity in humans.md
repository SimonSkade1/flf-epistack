## Why this is evidence
An interventional human coupling: raising fasting TMAO ~6-fold concurrently increased platelet responsiveness, a proposed pro-thrombotic causal mechanism. Favors H-16 (elevated TMAO is functionally active in humans) over H-45 (passive marker). Confounded by choline itself acting directly; the TMAO-specific attribution is strengthened by O-66's within-cohort TMAO-platelet correlation.
