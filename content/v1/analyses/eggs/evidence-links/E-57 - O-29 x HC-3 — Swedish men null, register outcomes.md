## Why this is evidence
A functionally-null European cohort with register-based (low-misclassification) outcomes is predicted by H-8 (Europe null) and H-9 (non-US null); it is evidence against H-51's uniform non-null effect. The register-based ascertainment weakens the measurement-error escape a uniform-effect defender could otherwise invoke.
