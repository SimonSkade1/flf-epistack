## Why this is evidence
The egg-CVD association reverses to null on adjustment for correlated lifestyle and co-consumed foods — not for dietary cholesterol — pointing to confounding (H-54). Contrast O-60, where the removal comes specifically from cholesterol adjustment (H-26): the two attenuations implicate different operative accounts.
