## Why this is evidence
A habitual dose (4 eggs/day, 4 weeks) doesn't raise fasting TMAO or platelet reactivity, undercutting the non-cholesterol choline->TMAO egg-component route of H-54. Consistent with H-26/H-3, under which egg-choline is not the operative pathway.
