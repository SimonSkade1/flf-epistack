## Why this is evidence
Dietary cholesterol from all sources carries a monotonic CVD/mortality dose-response, i.e. cholesterol per se (not egg per se) is the graded exposure — direct support for H-26. H-3 predicts cholesterol quantity is a minor lever; H-54 predicts even this cholesterol association is confounded.
