The SARS-CoV-2 furin cleavage site is created by a 12-nucleotide insert (TCCTCGGCGGGC) coding PRRA at the S1/S2 junction; the two adjacent arginines are encoded by CGG-CGG, a rare codon in these viruses (only ~5% of arginines use CGG, and this is the only doubled CGG in the genome), and the insert contains a FauI restriction site.
