At the Xinyuan live-animal market (Guangdong, January 2004), all 91 palm civets and all 15 raccoon dogs sampled tested RT-PCR positive for a SARS-CoV-like virus, and 22 of 24 environmental swabs (cages, walls, cash tables) were positive.
