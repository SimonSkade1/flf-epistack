The authors restrict the correlation to the 70 January-12 wildlife-stall samples (a balanced subset, all sequenced irrespective of positivity) rather than the full sequenced set. Human mtDNA does not track the virus; porcupine and marmot do (reflecting their concentration in stall A). Notably raccoon dog itself is not among the significantly positively-correlated species here. Same D-1 data that S-39 (Bloom) analyses with the opposite emphasis.
