Dating used recCA rooting (a reconstructed recombinant common ancestor across 15 non-recombinant sarbecovirus regions) as well as unconstrained rooting. Median lineage-B tMRCA 15 Dec, lineage-A tMRCA 20 Dec; lineage B primary case predated lineage A in 64.6% of the posterior, by a median 7 days.
