Because these viruses have narrow, known host ranges and were found where their host's mtDNA was, they indicate that live, productively infected animals of those species (raccoon dog, bamboo rat, civet) were physically present in the market in late 2019, and their phylogenetic links to southern-China (Guangxi/Yunnan) farms trace a wildlife-trade conduit into Wuhan. Recovered from the same D-1 metagenomic reads.
