First sampling (28 Apr-1 May): NB1 house A 2/3 positive (Ct 35.95, 38.18); NB1 house B 1/3 (Ct 35.03); NB2 1/3 (Ct 35.14). Second and third sampling moments (16 May) were all negative. Establishes an airborne exposure route for anyone in the sheds during the acute phase.
