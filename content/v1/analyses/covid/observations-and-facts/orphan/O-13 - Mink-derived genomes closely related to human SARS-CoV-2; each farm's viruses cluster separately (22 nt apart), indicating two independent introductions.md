Index sample plus 4 (NB1) and 5 (NB2) additional animals were sequenced (11 genomes total). Index genomes differed from Wuhan-Hu-1 by 9 (NB1) and 15 (NB2) substitutions; the two farm index sequences "diverge at 22 nucleotide positions, but the sequences from each farm cluster together", supporting "separate virus introductions to each of the farms". Within-farm sequence variation is present.
