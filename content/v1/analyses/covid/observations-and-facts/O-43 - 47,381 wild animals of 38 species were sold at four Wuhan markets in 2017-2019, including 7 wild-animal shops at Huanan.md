Surveys covered Baishazhou (2 shops), Huanan Seafood (7 shops), Dijiao pet market (4 shops) and Qiyimen live-animal market (4 shops), with vendor interviews on species, monthly quantities, prices and wild-caught-vs-farmed origin. The data predate the pandemic and were not collected with any origins agenda.
