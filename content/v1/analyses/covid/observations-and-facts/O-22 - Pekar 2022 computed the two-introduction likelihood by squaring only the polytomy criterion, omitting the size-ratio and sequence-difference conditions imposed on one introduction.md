Records the structure of P2022's calculation (from P2022's own simulations and supplement): I2's likelihood used only the polytomy sub-criterion tauP applied twice, while I1's likelihood additionally required the size-ratio and sequence-difference conditions. This asymmetry is the factual basis for Weissman's error argument.
