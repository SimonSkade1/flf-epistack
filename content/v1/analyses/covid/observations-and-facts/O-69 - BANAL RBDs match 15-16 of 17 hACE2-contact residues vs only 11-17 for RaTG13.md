Of the 17 RBD residues that contact human ACE2, 16/17 are conserved in BANAL-52/103 (one mismatch, H498Q) and 15/17 in BANAL-236 (K493Q, H498Q), versus only 13/17 for the Cambodian R. shameli virus and 11/17 for RaTG13 - the BANAL RBDs are far closer to SARS-CoV-2 at the receptor interface than RaTG13.
