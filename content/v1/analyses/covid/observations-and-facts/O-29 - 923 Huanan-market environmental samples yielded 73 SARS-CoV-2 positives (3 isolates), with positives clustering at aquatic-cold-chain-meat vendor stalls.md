The same China-CDC Huanan-market environmental sampling campaign later released metagenomically and re-analysed by S-35 (Crits-Christoph) and S-39 (Bloom) — hence D-1. Positives concentrate on surfaces of product-selling stalls and drains/sewers. The report frames the contamination as "compatible with introduction of the virus through infected people, infected animals or contaminated products", and its conclusion leans to human shedding.
