SARS-CoV-2 has a polybasic (furin) cleavage site formed by a 12-nucleotide insertion adding the amino acids PRRA (yielding an RRAR motif) at the S1/S2 boundary of the spike; such sites are absent in the related lineage B betacoronaviruses.
