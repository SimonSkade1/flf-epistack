Sequencing the spike gene across market civets and raccoon dogs defined 27 signature nucleotide variations (SNVs) forming a graded series: a prototype group with 0 SNVs (two raccoon dogs and one civet), market civets plus an early patient with 2-7 SNVs, and human epidemic strains with 16-27 SNVs.
