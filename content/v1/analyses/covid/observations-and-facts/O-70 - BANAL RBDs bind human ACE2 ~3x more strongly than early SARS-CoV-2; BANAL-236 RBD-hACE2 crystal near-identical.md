By biolayer interferometry, the BANAL-52/103 and BANAL-236 RBDs bind human ACE2 with a dissociation constant three times lower (higher affinity) than the early Wuhan SARS-CoV-2 RBD; the BANAL-236 RBD-hACE2 crystal structure (PDB 7PKI, 2.9 A) is essentially identical to SARS-CoV-2 (Ca RMSD 0.36 A).
