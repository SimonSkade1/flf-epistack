The polybasic furin site and its PRRA insert are absent from all related lineage B betacoronaviruses; the RmYN02 virus reported to carry a natural PAA insertion at S1/S2 shows, on nucleotide alignment to its closest relatives (bat-SL-CoVZC45/ZXC21), only substitutions and no actual insertion.
