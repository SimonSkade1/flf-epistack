Table 3 breakdown: Huanan market 327, warehouses 32, cats/rats/vectors 92, surrounding markets 6 — all 0 positive. Table 4 species include rabbit/hare (104), stray cat (80, incl. faeces), snake (80), hedgehog (67), muntjac (18), dog (17), badger (16, frozen carcasses, identity not DNA-barcoded), bamboo rat (15), etc. Critically, the report states sampling drew on "unsold goods kept in refrigerators and freezers" and vector animals — i.e. the live wildlife sold in December had already been removed at market closure (1 Jan 2020). The report also claims "no verified reports of live mammals being sold around 2019", which later market-metagenomic work disputes. Shares the China-CDC market-collection basis (D-1) with S-35 and S-39.
