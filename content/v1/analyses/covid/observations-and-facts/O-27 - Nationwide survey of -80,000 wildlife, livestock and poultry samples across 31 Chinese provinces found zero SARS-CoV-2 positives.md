The largest animal-surveillance effort reported to date on the origin question, channelled entirely through the China-CDC-gatekept joint report (hence D-10). No intermediate or reservoir host was identified anywhere in the Chinese animal population sampled. A negative of this kind is limited by absence-of-evidence: it constrains but cannot exclude an intermediate host, and the sampling frame/completeness cannot be independently verified.
