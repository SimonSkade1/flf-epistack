Three new sarbecoviruses were isolated from cave bats in northern Laos - BANAL-52 (Rhinolophus malayanus), BANAL-103 (R. pusillus) and BANAL-236 (R. marshalli); BANAL-52 is 96.8% identical to SARS-CoV-2 across the whole genome, higher than RaTG13's 96.1%, making it the closest known full-genome relative.
