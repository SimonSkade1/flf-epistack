The official Chinese CDC-led line-list analysis of the first 425 laboratory-confirmed cases (onset through 22 Jan 2020). Reports that 55% of cases with onset before 1 Jan 2020 had a Huanan market link, versus only 8.6% of cases with onset on or after 1 Jan 2020 — the key line-list statistic behind the "market association fades as the outbreak grows, consistent with human-to-human spread taking over from an initial market-centered spillover" argument. Also estimates a mean incubation period of 5.2 days and an early epidemic doubling time (~7.4 days), feeding downstream index-case-timing work.

relevance_note: the primary official line-list dataset (425 cases) from which the market-linkage percentages and the "declining market association over time" argument both derive.
