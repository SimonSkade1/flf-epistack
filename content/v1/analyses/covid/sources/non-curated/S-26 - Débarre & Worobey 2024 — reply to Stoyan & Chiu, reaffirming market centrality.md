Worobey's own rebuttal to Stoyan & Chiu (2024), titled "Confirmation of the centrality of the Huanan market among early COVID-19 cases." Re-runs the geospatial analysis using alternative statistical approaches intended to sidestep Stoyan & Chiu's specific objections (centroid assumption, Monte Carlo test construction), and argues the market-centered clustering signal in the December-2019 case data survives those methodological objections.
