Declassified 23 June 2023 under the COVID-19 Origin Act, this report specifically addresses whether WIV researchers, samples, or genetic-engineering activity connect to COVID-19's origin. It reaffirms the IC's persistent split (four agencies + NIC favoring natural origin at low confidence; FBI at moderate confidence and DOE at low confidence favoring a lab-associated incident, consistent with the Feb-2023 updates — companion node). It states the IC has no indication WIV possessed or worked with SARS-CoV-2, or a close progenitor virus, before the outbreak began, while separately noting that a small number of WIV researchers were ill in autumn 2019 with symptoms consistent with, but not diagnostic of, COVID-19 or several common illnesses.

relevance_note: The most recent whole-of-IC statement specifically on the WIV link; narrows and adds caveats to the broader Jan-2021 State Department fact-sheet claims (companion node).
