Section "恶意诽谤21" ("Malicious Slander #21") of this compiled PRC Embassy rebuttal document directly addresses Jesse Bloom's 2021 report that Wuhan-University-generated early-outbreak sequencing reads had been deleted from the NCBI SRA database. States that Chinese authorities investigated and found the withdrawal originated with the Wuhan University research team itself: the journal (Small) had removed the manuscript's data-availability paragraph during editing, the researchers judged the deposited reads no longer necessary given the paper no longer described them, and requested NIH withdraw the data on 16 June 2021 — not a coordinated cover-up. Echoes and formalizes in writing the same account given orally by Vice-Minister Zeng Yixin at the 22 July 2021 State Council Information Office press conference.
