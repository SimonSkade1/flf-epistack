The primary video record of the ~18-20 hour, 3-part, $100,000 judged debate (Nov 2023) between Saar Wilf/Yuri Deigin for Rootclaim (lab leak) and Peter Miller (zoonosis): part 1 is zoonosis-side opening arguments (33.5k views as of 2026-07-23), part 2 is lab-leak-side opening arguments (8.6k views), part 3 is discussion and judges' questions (7.9k views); all uploaded 2023-12-30 to a YouTube channel credited to Peter Miller. Judged independently by Will van Treuren and Eric Stansifer, both of whom ruled for zoonosis. This video record, plus the ~750 slides of written supplementary material referenced in the judges' decisions, is the base debate material that every other node in this slice (Rootclaim's posts, the judges' decisions, Scott Alexander's ACX review, Weissman's and Filan's analyses) discusses, rebuts, or formalizes.
