Peer-reviewed version of [[S-15 - China CDC Research Square preprint — Huanan market environmental & animal surveillance]] (Nature 631:402–408). Confirms 74/923 environmental samples positive (70 RT-qPCR + 4 by Bowtie2 read mapping), 0/457 animal-source samples positive, ~87.5% of positives concentrated in the market's west wing (wildlife stalls), and recovers 4 near-complete SARS-CoV-2 genomes from environmental swabs (labelled A20 = lineage A; B5, F13, F54 = lineage B), both found at the market. This is the paper whose raw sequencing reads (not originally released alongside it) were later uploaded to GISAID/NGDC in Mar 2023, triggering the Crits-Christoph/Débarre reanalyses.
relevance_note: the canonical peer-reviewed China CDC market-environmental dataset; nearly every other node in this slice reanalyzes or critiques this one.
