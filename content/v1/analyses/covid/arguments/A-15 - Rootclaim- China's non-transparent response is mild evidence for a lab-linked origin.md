## reasoning
China's official response was not transparent — restricting WHO access, destroying samples, and withholding information — which could be an attempt to hide evidence of lab-blame, though Rootclaim notes such secrecy is not particularly surprising even under a purely natural origin. China also deployed Major General Chen Wei (Academy of Military Medical Sciences) to oversee COVID work at the WIV, which could hint at bioweapon involvement but is judged probably immaterial. Factors: lab-escape ×1.5, zoonotic-collection ×1.5. Running likelihoods: Zoonotic 1.4%, Lab-escape 94%, Bioweapon 2.4%, Zoonotic-collection 2.3%.

## Validity verdict (step 6)
Reconstruction — Premise: China's response was non-transparent (restricted WHO access, destroyed samples, withheld information; a military scientist installed at WIV). Load-bearing step: such secrecy is somewhat more expected if there is a lab link to conceal than under a purely natural origin, so it is mild evidence for a lab-linked origin.

Probe for an undercutting defeater: the obvious one — an authoritarian state conceals reflexively regardless of origin — is already built into the argument, which acknowledges the secrecy "is not particularly surprising even under a purely natural origin" and therefore claims only *mild* evidence (×1.5, not larger). That defeater caps the strength but leaves a residual asymmetry intact, so the direction holds. This is a textbook weak-but-valid directional update. status: approved; reason_if_not_false: checked.
