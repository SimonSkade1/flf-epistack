question: When and by what route did SARS-CoV-2 acquire its furin cleavage site, given its absence in the closest bat relatives?
---

Because the closest wild relatives all lack a furin cleavage site, SARS-CoV-2 acquired its FCS by a distinct later event whose route (natural or otherwise) is not accounted for by these bat genomes.
