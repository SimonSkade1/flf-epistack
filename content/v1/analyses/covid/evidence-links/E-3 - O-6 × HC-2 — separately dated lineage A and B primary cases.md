## Why this is evidence

The clock dates lineage A's and lineage B's primary infections as two distinct, near-simultaneous events rather than one lineage budding off the other later. H-5 predicts exactly such separately dated primary cases; H-16 predicts a single human MRCA from which both lineages descend, with no second independently dated primary case. The dating is model-laden, so the discrimination is weak, but the members read it differently.
