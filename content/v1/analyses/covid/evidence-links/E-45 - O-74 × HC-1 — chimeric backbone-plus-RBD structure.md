## Why this is evidence
A RaTG13-like backbone carrying a pangolin-like RBD is read as a laboratory chimera (favouring H-43); the natural members explain the same backbone/RBD split by recombination (O-73). Discriminates H-43 from H-41/H-42, direction contested.
