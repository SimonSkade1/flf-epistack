## Why this is evidence
The polybasic furin site — the one human-adapting feature absent from close lineage-B relatives — is the crux the members split on: H-43 predicts an inserted/engineered site, H-41/H-42 a naturally acquired one. Its flanking O-glycans (O-61) and non-relative status (O-72/O-77) are read oppositely by the two sides; direction contested, but it discriminates H-43 from the natural members.
