## Why this is evidence

H-5 (two or more introductions) reads two co-dominant early lineages separated by a clean two-linked-mutation gap as a topology that single-introduction epidemic simulations reproduce only rarely, so the structure favours separate introductions. H-16 (single introduction) explains the same two-lineage structure as ordinary within-human diversification from one seeding and disputes that the topology is improbable under one introduction. The members predict this structural fact differently.
