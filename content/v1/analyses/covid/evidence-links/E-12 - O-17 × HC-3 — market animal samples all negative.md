## Why this is evidence
A wildlife-origin market spillover (H-14) prima facie predicts some infected animals at the market; a human/cold-chain-seeded amplifier (H-15) predicts animal samples negative. All-negative animal sampling is prima facie against H-14 relative to H-15 — but the samples post-date removal of the live animals (attached caveat), which neutralizes it.
