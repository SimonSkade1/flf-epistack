## Why this is evidence
H-43 (engineered) predicts a rationally chosen human-ACE2 interface; that five of the six SARS-CoV-2 contact residues differ from SARS-CoV — the characterised template — and instead match a natural pangolin donor (O-64) is what the natural genome of H-41/H-42 predicts. Discriminates H-43 from the two natural members; H-41 and H-42 share this genome and are not separated by it.
