## Why this is evidence
Positives concentrating at aquatic/cold-chain/meat stalls rather than the wildlife stalls fits an externally-introduced (cold-chain or human-shed) amplifier (H-15) better than a live-wildlife spillover concentrated at wildlife stalls (H-14).
