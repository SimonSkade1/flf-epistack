## Why this is evidence
Genomes of narrow-host-range wildlife viruses co-located with their hosts show live, productively infected susceptible animals (raccoon dog, civet) were present via the wildlife trade — a precondition H-14 needs and H-15/H-19 do not, and against the 'no live mammals sold' premise underlying the amplifier/artifact readings.
