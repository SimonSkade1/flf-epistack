Used by the paper as its flagship historical example of *model* (as opposed to theory or calculation) failure: the underlying theory of the relevant nuclear reactions was understood, but the model of which reactions mattered was too narrow. Fallout affected the Marshall Islands and killed a Japanese fisherman.
