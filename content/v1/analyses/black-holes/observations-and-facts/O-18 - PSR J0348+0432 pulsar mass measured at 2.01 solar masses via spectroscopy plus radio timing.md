Model dependence is confined to the white-dwarf mass step: cooling tracks with thick hydrogen envelopes (MESA models); the paper argues a thin-envelope alternative would require anomalous fine-tuning and a ~20 Myr cooling age inconsistent with the system. Timing: 8,121 TOAs from GBT, Arecibo, and Effelsberg; assuming GR, the intersection of q and orbital decay gives the consistent M_PSR = 2.07 +0.20/-0.21 Msun.
