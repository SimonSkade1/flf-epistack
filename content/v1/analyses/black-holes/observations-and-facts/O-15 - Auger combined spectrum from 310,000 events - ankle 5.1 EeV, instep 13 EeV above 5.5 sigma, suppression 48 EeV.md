The dominant systematic is the 14% absolute energy-scale uncertainty common to all Auger flux results (calorimetric fluorescence calibration); bin-to-bin flux correlations from it are 0.75-0.99 (Table VI). Combined-spectrum normalization J0 = 1.271 +/- 0.004 km^-2 sr^-1 yr^-1 EeV^-1 with indices gamma1 = 3.26, gamma2 = 2.51, gamma3 = 2.99 (Table I).
