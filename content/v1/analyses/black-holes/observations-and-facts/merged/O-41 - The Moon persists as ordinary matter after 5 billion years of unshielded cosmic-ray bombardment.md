The target fact of the lunar strangelet bound. The paper notes why the Moon rather than Earth or stars: bodies with atmospheres or gaseous envelopes present cosmic rays overwhelmingly with light nuclei (H, He), which are not a plausible way to make strange matter, whereas bare lunar rock supplies heavy-ion targets directly.
