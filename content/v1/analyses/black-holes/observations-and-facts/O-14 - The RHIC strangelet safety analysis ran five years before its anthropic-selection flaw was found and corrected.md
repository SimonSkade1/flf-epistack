The corrected analysis (anthropic bias removed, using Jaffe et al. 2000 inputs plus a planetary-formation-rate model) still bounded accelerator risk below 1e-12/yr, but the episode is a documented instance of a serious flaw persisting undetected in exactly the class of physics safety argument at issue for the LHC. The paper also notes Kent (2004) found mistakes in how risk probabilities were stated across versions of the Dar et al. paper and the Brookhaven report.
