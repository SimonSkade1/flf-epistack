Bondi flags both parts as plausible rather than proven: the steady-state equations do not select λ (an energy-comparison argument does), and the interpolation formula for the intermediate velocity-and-pressure-limited regime is an order-of-magnitude conjecture. He also notes the pressure limitation is likely somewhat overestimated if the gas can radiate compression heat (effective γ closer to 1, raising the rate).
