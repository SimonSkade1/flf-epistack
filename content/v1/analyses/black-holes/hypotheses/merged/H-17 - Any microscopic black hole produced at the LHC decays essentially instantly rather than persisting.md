The report treats stability as requiring a violation of basic principles: suppressing the decay rate relative to the production rate would violate quantum mechanics, and suppressing Hawking radiation would violate general relativity (both mechanisms remain valid in the extra-dimensional scenarios that motivate black-hole production). The stable case is nevertheless analysed separately as a worst case, via charge stopping and the Giddings-Mangano accretion bounds.
