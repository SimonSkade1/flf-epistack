Proposes a single warped (non-factorizable, AdS5-sliced) extra dimension bounded by two branes as an alternative solution to the hierarchy problem: the exponential warp factor between the branes generates the weak/Planck hierarchy from O(1) input parameters, without requiring large extra-dimension volumes. arXiv:hep-ph/9905221. Together with ADD, this is the other principal TeV-scale-gravity model family invoked in collider black-hole-production phenomenology.
relevance_note: second (warped-geometry) branch of the TeV-scale-gravity models that license collider black holes.
