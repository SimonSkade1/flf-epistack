Sets the quantitative theoretical bar any "could a strangelet convert Earth" argument has to clear.
relevance_note: the quantitative companion to Witten 1984, defining exactly when/whether strange matter is the true ground state — load-bearing for whether strangelet conversion is even conceivable.
