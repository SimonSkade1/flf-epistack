The full derivation underlying "Hawking radiation": using QFT on a fixed classical collapsing-star background, shows particle creation gives a black hole a thermal emission spectrum at temperature T = κ/2π (surface gravity), causing gradual mass loss and eventual evaporation. Erratum in Commun. Math. Phys. 46 (1976) 206. This is the single most-cited theoretical basis for the claim that any LHC-produced micro black hole would decay rather than persist — i.e., the entire content of "Premise A."
