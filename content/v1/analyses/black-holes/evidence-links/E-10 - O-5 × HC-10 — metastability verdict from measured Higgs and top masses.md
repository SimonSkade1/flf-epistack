## Why this is evidence
The computed verdict is metastability — a decay channel exists but with enormous lifetime. H-31 predicts exactly this outcome; under H-42 the same inputs would have to yield absolute stability, so the observation is what H-42 says should not happen (again conditional on the SM effective-potential extrapolation holding to high scales).
