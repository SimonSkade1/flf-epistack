## Why this is evidence

A carefully checked, high-stakes, hard-physics safety-relevant calculation failed not through bad theory or arithmetic but through a too-narrow model of which processes mattered — the failure mode internal rigor cannot catch. H-11 predicts such historical failures exist even in the most scrutinized nuclear-weapons-grade work; under H-46's picture of robust expert safety argumentation, a headline model failure of this kind in exactly this genre of calculation is more surprising.
