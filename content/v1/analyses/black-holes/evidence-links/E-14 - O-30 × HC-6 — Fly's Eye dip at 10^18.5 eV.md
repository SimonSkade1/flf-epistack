## Why this is evidence
Both H-21 (Galactic-heavy to extragalactic-light transition at the break) and H-9 (pair-production dip of extragalactic protons) predict a dip at about this energy, and its confirmation by Akeno, Haverah Park and Yakutsk is unlikely under the instrumental branch of H-37 — so this raises H-9 and H-21 jointly against the residual, without separating the two from each other.
