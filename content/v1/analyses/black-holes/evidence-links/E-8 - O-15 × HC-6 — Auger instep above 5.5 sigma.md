## Why this is evidence
The instep is the discriminator: H-12 predicts a feature between ankle and suppression from the crossover of helium and CNO components, while a pure extragalactic-proton spectrum (H-9) and a Galactic-to-extragalactic transition (H-21) predict a featureless power law there. The instep's establishment above 5.5 sigma is expected under H-12 and surprising under H-9 and H-21.
