## Why this is evidence

A concrete, independently dated instance of the class the neutron-star bound needs: a massive neutron star that has persisted for gigayears with a long-lived binary companion (the companion providing the beam-dump exposure that circumvents magnetic screening on the production side). Its survival is expected under H-22 and improbable under H-8-type fast-accretion physics applied to a hole captured in supranuclear-density matter, where accretion is fastest of all. Same discrimination direction as the general neutron-star-population observation, from a distinct measurement.
