## Why this is evidence

The diagnostic content is the phase-diagram placement, not the lifetime: the measured parameters fall inside the narrow metastable wedge between absolute stability and rapid instability. H-6 predicts landing near the critical line — of which the metastable-but-long-lived verdict is the concrete realization; H-43 assigns the wedge no special probability. The lifetime figure itself (vastly exceeding the age of the universe) is predicted equally by both members and carries no weight here.
