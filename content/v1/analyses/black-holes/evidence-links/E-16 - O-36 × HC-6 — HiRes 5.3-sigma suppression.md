## Why this is evidence
H-9 predicts a suppression at just this energy from photo-pion losses of protons on the CMB; the instrumental branch of H-37 (and the earlier AGASA unbroken spectrum this contradicts) predicts none. H-12 also yields a steepening, but as a source property whose energy need not coincide with the GZK scale — so the location as well as the existence of the break discriminates.
