## Why this is evidence
H-20 is precisely the prediction this search was built to test: solar-captured GUT monopoles catalyzing proton decay would produce a solar-correlated 19-55 MeV neutrino excess at Super-K. H-20 predicts a signal (for flux and catalysis cross section in the astrophysically relevant range); H-41 predicts the observed null. The absence of any significant solar-direction excess in 2853 live days therefore favours H-41 over H-20 to the extent the scenario's flux would have been detectable.
