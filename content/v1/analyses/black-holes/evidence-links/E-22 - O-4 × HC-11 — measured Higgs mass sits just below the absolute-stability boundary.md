## Why this is evidence

The measured Mh lands about two combined sigma below the critical stability value — close enough that absolute stability is only marginally excluded. H-6 predicts the parameters cluster near the phase boundary, so a Higgs mass within a few GeV of the critical value is expected; under H-43 the mass could have fallen anywhere in its allowed range, making such proximity an unforced coincidence.
