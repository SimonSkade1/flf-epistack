## Why this is evidence
The X_max of the event matches a proton shower, which is expected under the protonic accounts (H-9, H-21) and disfavored under H-12's helium/CNO composition at the highest energies. Separately, one event a factor of several above the suppression energy is more surprising under a sharp source-rigidity cutoff (H-12) than under GZK propagation from a possibly nearby source (H-9).
