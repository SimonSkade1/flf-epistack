## Why this is evidence
Three technique- and hemisphere-independent experiments seeing the same structure is very likely under any physical account (H-9, H-12, H-21) and unlikely under the shared-instrumental-artifact branch of H-37. The residual 3-sigma break-energy difference cuts the other way for a *common* energy-scale artifact, and mildly favors accounts (H-12-style source astrophysics) that allow the northern and southern skies to differ.
