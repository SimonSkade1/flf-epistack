Group R's continental compilation of screened late-Quaternary ages for Australian megafauna and archaeological occupation.

`known_biases`: the screening and site-selection rules are group R's; everything resting on this compilation moves together if the screen is systematically too permissive or too strict. It is the shared basis behind [[S-1 - Re-dated last-appearance ages for Sahul's extinct megafauna]], [[S-5 - Speleothem aridity record for the Sahul interior, 60-30 ka]] (age model) and [[S-23 - Popular-science synthesis of Australian megafauna extinctions]] — which is why the observations resting on it must be judged **jointly**, not as independent witnesses.
