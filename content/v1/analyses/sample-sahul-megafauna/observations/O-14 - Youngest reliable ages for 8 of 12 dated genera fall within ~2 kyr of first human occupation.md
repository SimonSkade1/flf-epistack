The tight clustering of youngest reliable ages against the first-occupation window is the single most-cited pattern in the debate. It rests on the screened ages in [[D-4 - Continental late-Quaternary age compilation, group R]] (through [[S-1 - Re-dated last-appearance ages for Sahul's extinct megafauna]]), so it must be judged **jointly** with [[O-22 - Three megafauna genera persist more than 5 kyr after first human arrival]], which rests on the same compilation — see [[E-14 - O-14 × HC-1 +A-7 — youngest ages cluster in the arrival window]].
