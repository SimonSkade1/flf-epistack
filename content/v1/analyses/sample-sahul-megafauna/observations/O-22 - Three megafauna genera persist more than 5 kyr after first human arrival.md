Three genera survive well past first human arrival — the counter-pattern to [[O-14 - Youngest reliable ages for 8 of 12 dated genera fall within ~2 kyr of first human occupation]]. A rapid kill-off predicts few or no such survivors; a staggered habitat squeeze predicts exactly this refugial tail. Rests on the same screened compilation [[D-4 - Continental late-Quaternary age compilation, group R]] as O-14, so the two are one witness, not two — estimated jointly in [[E-14 - O-14 × HC-1 +A-7 — youngest ages cluster in the arrival window]].
