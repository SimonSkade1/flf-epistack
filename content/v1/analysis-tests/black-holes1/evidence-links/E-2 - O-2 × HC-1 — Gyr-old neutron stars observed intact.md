## Why this is evidence

H-1 predicts cosmic-ray-produced stable black holes stopped in neutron stars would have consumed them within 10^8-10^9 yr; H-2 predicts survival. Intact old neutron stars are surprising under H-1, expected under H-2 (modulo magnetosphere-penetration assumptions).
