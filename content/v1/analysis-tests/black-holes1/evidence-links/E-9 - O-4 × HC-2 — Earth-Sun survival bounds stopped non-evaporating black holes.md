## Why this is evidence

Under H-7, any cosmic-ray-produced long-lived black hole that stops in Earth or Sun (charged/EM-interacting variants) would have had 4.5 Gyr to accrete; survival disfavors those H-7 sub-cases. Under H-4 survival is certain. Neutral fast black holes escape, so the bound covers only the stopped sub-cases.
