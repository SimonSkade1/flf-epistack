## Why this is evidence

Under H-7, cosmic-ray-produced long-lived black holes are stopped by NS density (~10^14 g/cm^3) and would accrete the star; 10^8-10^9 yr survival is then unexpected. Under H-4 survival is certain. Weaker than the WD bound (magnetosphere assumptions).
