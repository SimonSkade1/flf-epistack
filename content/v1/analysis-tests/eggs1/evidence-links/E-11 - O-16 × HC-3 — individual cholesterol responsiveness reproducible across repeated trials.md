## Why this is evidence
The core discriminator for H-7: a stable within-person classification into hypo- and hyperresponders across three alternating trials is what H-7 predicts and what H-13 (homogeneous effect, so between-trial differences should be measurement noise and not reproducible) does not. H-6 makes no prediction here.
