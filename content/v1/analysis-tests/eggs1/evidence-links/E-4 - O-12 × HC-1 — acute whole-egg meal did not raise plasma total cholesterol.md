## Why this is evidence

H-5 predicts a blunted post-meal cholesterol response because little egg cholesterol enters circulation. H-1 predicts a measurable rise. H-8 is weakly predictive (saturation is about chronic dose, not an acute meal), H-11's no-effect variant also predicts the null.
