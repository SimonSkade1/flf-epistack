## Why this is evidence

Per-member (H-2, H-3, H-4, H-9, H-10, H-12):
1. H-2: disconfirmed - the one protective signal in PURE fails external replication, so protective findings elsewhere (e.g. CKB) look more like population-specific noise.
2. H-3: unengaged (no harm shown either).
3. H-4: mildly predicted (apparent inverse signals are artefactual).
4. H-9: predicted.
5. H-10: predicted - non-replication is what a true global null generates.
6. H-12: mildly disconfirmed - a real direction-varying effect would more often replicate within a matched patient population.
