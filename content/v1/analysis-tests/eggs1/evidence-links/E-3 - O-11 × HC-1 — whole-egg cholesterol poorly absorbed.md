## Why this is evidence

This is H-5's defining mechanism claim, so H-5 predicts it strongly. H-8 attributes the small effect to a saturating hepatic/homeostatic dose-response rather than to malabsorption, so it does not predict poor absorption. H-1 predicts adequate absorption sufficient to drive an ApoB rise.
