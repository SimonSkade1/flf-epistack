## Why this is evidence
H-7 specifies a *lifestyle-independent* responder trait; the absence of correlation with habitual cholesterol intake or body weight is what that predicts, while a lifestyle-driven (hence non-intrinsic) account would expect such correlations. H-13 predicts no meaningful responsiveness axis to correlate with anything.
