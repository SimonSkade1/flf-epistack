## Why this is evidence

Per-member (H-2, H-3, H-4, H-9, H-10, H-12):
1. H-2: strongly predicted (mortality-level protection).
2. H-3: predicts the opposite sign on mortality.
3. H-4: scoped to IHD, so only weakly engaged; a CVD-mortality inverse is mildly against its null.
4. H-9: out of scope (non-Western), near-uninformative.
5. H-10: in scope and disconfirmed - an 18% CVD-mortality difference is a material effect.
6. H-12: compatible.
