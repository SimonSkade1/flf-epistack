## Why this is evidence

H-5 explicitly predicts the furin cleavage site is absent from the natural relatives because it was inserted; H-1 and H-6 must have it arise naturally and so predict, less strongly, that close relatives would sometimes carry one. Favours H-5, though only weakly given how sparsely the sarbecovirus reservoir is sampled.
