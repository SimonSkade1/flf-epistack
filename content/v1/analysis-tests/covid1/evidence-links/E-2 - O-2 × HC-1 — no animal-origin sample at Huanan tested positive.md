## Why this is evidence

H-1 is the only member that predicts an infected animal could have been present at the market at all, so it alone assigns non-trivial probability to a positive animal sample; H-5 and H-6 predict zero positives with near-certainty. The strength of the miss is heavily discounted by the sampling timeline (O-3), which is why that caveat is carried by an argument rather than by this edge.
