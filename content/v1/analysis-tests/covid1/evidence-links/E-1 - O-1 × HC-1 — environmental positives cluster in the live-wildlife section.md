## Why this is evidence

H-1 predicts contamination to be densest exactly where live susceptible wildlife was sold and slaughtered. H-5 and H-6 predict market contamination only as a downstream human-amplification event, which should track human footfall/stall density rather than the live-wildlife section specifically, and gives no reason for a spatial gradient toward that section.
