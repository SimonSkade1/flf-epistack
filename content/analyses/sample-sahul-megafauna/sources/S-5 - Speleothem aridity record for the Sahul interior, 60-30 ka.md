A U-Th-dated speleothem oxygen-isotope record for the Sahul interior across 60–30 ka. The aridity maximum it records sits several kyr *after* the first-occupation window, weakening a tight climate–extinction coincidence.

`relevance_note`: the main independent test of whether aridification timing lines up with the extinction pulse.
