The "overkill" answer: a naive prey fauna is hunted to collapse within a few millennia of human arrival. Predicts the youngest ages pile up **inside** the arrival window ([[O-14 - Youngest reliable ages for 8 of 12 dated genera fall within ~2 kyr of first human occupation]]) and struggles to explain long-persisting survivors ([[O-22 - Three megafauna genera persist more than 5 kyr after first human arrival]]).
