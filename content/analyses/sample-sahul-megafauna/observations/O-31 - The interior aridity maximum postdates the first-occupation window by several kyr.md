The timing test for the climate hypothesis. If aridification drove the extinction, the aridity maximum should coincide with the pulse; here it postdates the first-occupation window by several kyr.

> [!note] Independent basis — `data_basis` override
> [[S-5 - Speleothem aridity record for the Sahul interior, 60-30 ka]] draws its **age model** from the shared compilation [[D-4 - Continental late-Quaternary age compilation, group R]], but the aridity signal itself is a direct δ18O measurement on the source's own speleothem instrument [[D-7 - Speleothem oxygen-isotope instrument record (S-5)]]. This observation therefore carries a `data_basis` override to `D-7`, so step 5 does **not** flag it correlated with the D-4 dating pair — it is genuinely independent evidence. See [[E-31 - O-31 × HC-1 — aridity maximum postdates the arrival window]].
