Background fact orienting the chronology: at the ages the extinction debate turns on, radiocarbon is near its ceiling, so the load-bearing dates depend on optically-stimulated luminescence and U-series methods and on the screening choices in [[D-4 - Continental late-Quaternary age compilation, group R]].
