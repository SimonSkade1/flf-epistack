The oxygen-isotope (δ18O) record measured on the single speleothem instrument behind [[S-5 - Speleothem aridity record for the Sahul interior, 60-30 ka]].

`known_biases`: one cave, one growth axis — the aridity signal is regional, not continental, and any calibration or hiatus artefact in this one archive moves every δ18O-derived observation together. Distinct from [[D-4 - Continental late-Quaternary age compilation, group R]]: D-4 is the shared *dating* compilation, D-7 is this paper's own *proxy* instrument, which is why [[O-31 - The interior aridity maximum postdates the first-occupation window by several kyr]] counts as independent of the D-4 dating pair.
